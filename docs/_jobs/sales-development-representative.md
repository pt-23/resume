## THE ROLE

We are seeking a **Sales Development Representative** to join our growing sales team. You'll be responsible for generating qualified leads, building pipeline, and setting up our Account Executives for success. This is an excellent opportunity to launch or advance your sales career in a high-growth SaaS environment.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Lead Generation:** Identify and qualify potential customers through research and outreach
* **Outbound Prospecting:** Execute multi-channel outreach campaigns via email, phone, and social media
* **Lead Qualification:** Conduct discovery calls to understand prospect needs and qualify opportunities
* **Pipeline Building:** Generate qualified meetings and opportunities for Account Executives
* **CRM Management:** Maintain accurate records and activity tracking in Salesforce
* **Market Research:** Research target accounts and identify key decision makers
* **Collaboration:** Work closely with marketing and sales teams to optimize lead conversion
