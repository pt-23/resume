## THE ROLE

We are seeking a **Senior Accountant** to join our growing finance team. You'll play a critical role in maintaining accurate financial records, supporting month-end close processes, and ensuring compliance as we scale our operations globally. This is an excellent opportunity to contribute to the financial backbone of a rapidly growing unicorn company.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Financial Reporting:** Prepare accurate monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements
* **Month-End Close:** Lead month-end close activities and ensure timely completion
* **Revenue Recognition:** Support revenue accounting processes for SaaS subscriptions and enterprise contracts
* **Account Reconciliation:** Perform detailed account reconciliations and resolve discrepancies
* **Compliance:** Ensure adherence to GAAP and support audit processes
* **Process Improvement:** Identify opportunities to streamline accounting processes and implement best practices
* **Cross-functional Support:** Collaborate with sales, operations, and legal teams on financial matters

## REQUIREMENTS

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field

**Experience:**
* 5+ years of accounting experience, preferably in SaaS or technology companies
* Experience with month-end close processes and financial reporting
* Knowledge of revenue recognition standards (ASC 606)

**Technical Skills:**
* **ERP Systems:** Experience with NetSuite, QuickBooks, or similar accounting software
* **Excel:** Advanced Excel skills including pivot tables, VLOOKUP, and financial modeling
* **Financial Systems:** Familiarity with Salesforce, billing systems, and financial integrations

**Professional Qualifications:**
* CPA certification preferred
* Strong understanding of GAAP principles
* Experience with SOX compliance is a plus

**Soft Skills:**
* Exceptional attention to detail and accuracy
* Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities

## PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience with high-growth SaaS companies
* Knowledge of international accounting standards
* Experience with equity accounting and stock-based compensation
* Familiarity with data analytics and business intelligence tools
