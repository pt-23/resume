Placer.ai is transforming how organizations understand the physical world. Our location analytics platform provides unprecedented visibility into locations, markets, and consumer behavior. Placer empowers thousands of customers—from Fortune 500 companies to local governments and nonprofits—to make smarter, data-driven decisions.

**What sets us apart?**

## THE ROLE

We are seeking a **Content Marketing Specialist** to create compelling content that showcases the power of location intelligence. You'll develop content across multiple channels to educate our audience, drive brand awareness, and support our sales and marketing efforts.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Content Creation:** Develop blog posts, whitepapers, case studies, and thought leadership content
* **Social Media:** Manage social media content strategy and execution across platforms
* **Email Marketing:** Create engaging email campaigns and newsletters
* **Video Content:** Collaborate on video content including webinars and product demos
* **SEO Optimization:** Ensure content is optimized for search engines and discoverability
* **Content Strategy:** Develop content calendars and editorial strategies
* **Performance Analysis:** Track content performance and optimize based on data insights

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism, or related field

**Experience:**
* 3+ years of content marketing experience, preferably in B2B SaaS

* **Writing:** Exceptional writing and editing abilities across formats
* **SEO:** Understanding of SEO best practices and keyword research

**Technical Skills:**
