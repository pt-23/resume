## ABOUT PLACER.AI

Placer.ai is transforming how organizations understand the physical world. Our location analytics platform provides unprecedented visibility into locations, markets, and consumer behavior. Placer empowers thousands of customers—from Fortune 500 companies to local governments and nonprofits—to make smarter, data-driven decisions.

**What sets us apart?**
* **Innovation & Privacy:** We've built the most advanced location intelligence platform while maintaining an uncompromising commitment to privacy.
* **Rapid Growth:** We reached **$100M in annual recurring revenue** within 6 years, achieved **unicorn status ($1B+ valuation)** in 2022, and continue to expand as one of North America's fastest-growing tech companies.
* **Recognition:** Named one of *Forbes America's Best Startup Employers* and a *Deloitte Technology Fast 500* company.

## THE ROLE

We are seeking an **Enterprise Account Executive** to join our high-performing sales team. You'll be responsible for driving revenue growth by selling our location intelligence platform to Fortune 500 companies across retail, real estate, and other industries that rely on location-based insights.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Revenue Generation:** Achieve and exceed quarterly and annual sales targets
* **Enterprise Sales:** Manage complex, multi-stakeholder sales cycles with large enterprise accounts
* **Relationship Building:** Develop and maintain relationships with C-level executives and decision makers
* **Solution Selling:** Understand customer needs and position our platform as the solution
* **Pipeline Management:** Maintain accurate forecasting and pipeline management in CRM
* **Market Development:** Identify new opportunities and expand within existing accounts
* **Collaboration:** Work closely with marketing, customer success, and product teams
