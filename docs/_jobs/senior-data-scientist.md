## THE ROLE

We are seeking a **Senior Data Scientist** to join our growing data science team. You'll work with massive location datasets to build advanced machine learning models and analytics solutions that power insights for thousands of businesses worldwide. This role offers the opportunity to work on challenging problems at the intersection of location intelligence, consumer behavior, and business analytics.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Model Development:** Design and implement machine learning models for location analytics, consumer behavior prediction, and business intelligence
* **Data Analysis:** Analyze large-scale location datasets to identify patterns, trends, and actionable insights
* **Algorithm Design:** Develop novel algorithms for location data processing, privacy-preserving analytics, and real-time insights
* **Product Integration:** Collaborate with engineering teams to integrate ML models into production systems
* **Research & Innovation:** Stay current with latest developments in data science, ML, and location analytics
* **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Work with product managers and business teams to translate requirements into technical solutions
* **Mentorship:** Guide junior data scientists and contribute to team knowledge sharing

## REQUIREMENTS

**Education:** Master's or PhD in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics, or related quantitative field

**Experience:**
* 5+ years of experience in data science or machine learning roles
* 2+ years working with large-scale datasets (TB+ scale)
* Experience with location/geospatial data is a plus

**Technical Skills:**
* **Programming:** Expert-level Python, proficiency in SQL, experience with R or Scala
* **ML Frameworks:** TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, XGBoost
* **Data Tools:** Pandas, NumPy, Spark, Hadoop, Airflow
* **Cloud Platforms:** AWS, GCP, or Azure experience
* **Databases:** Experience with both SQL and NoSQL databases

**Statistical & ML Expertise:**
* Strong foundation in statistics, probability, and experimental design
* Experience with supervised and unsupervised learning techniques
* Knowledge of time series analysis and forecasting
* Familiarity with deep learning and neural networks
* Experience with A/B testing and causal inference

**Soft Skills:**
* Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
* Excellent communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences
* Ability to work in fast-paced, collaborative environment

## PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience with geospatial analysis and GIS tools
* Knowledge of privacy-preserving analytics techniques
* Experience with real-time ML systems and streaming data
* Background in retail, real estate, or consumer analytics
