## THE ROLE

We are seeking a **Financial Analyst** to join our finance team and support strategic planning and analysis. You'll work closely with leadership to provide insights that drive key business decisions, from pricing strategies to investment priorities. This role offers exposure to all aspects of SaaS financial operations in a high-growth environment.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

* **Financial Planning:** Support annual budgeting and quarterly forecasting processes
* **Business Analysis:** Analyze key metrics including ARR, churn, CAC, and LTV
* **Reporting:** Create executive dashboards and financial reports for leadership team
* **Variance Analysis:** Investigate budget vs. actual variances and provide explanations
* **Strategic Projects:** Support M&A analysis, pricing studies, and business case development
* **Data Analysis:** Work with large datasets to identify trends and business insights
* **Cross-functional Partnership:** Collaborate with sales, marketing, and operations teams
