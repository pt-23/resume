## SUMMARY
We are seeking an experienced **Director Engineer** with deep GenAI expertise to lead our GenAI product solutions and development efforts. In this role, you will drive the technical vision and architecture of our AI-powered location intelligence platform, building production-ready solutions that transform how businesses leverage location data. 

You will oversee full-stack development—from LLM integration and agentic workflows to real-time streaming interfaces—while establishing the technical foundation for our next-generation business intelligence tools.

---

## RESPONSIBILITIES
* **Technical Vision:** Lead architecture decisions for GenAI products, ensuring scalability and performance.
* **Roadmapping:** Establish technical roadmaps aligned with business objectives and scalability requirements.
* **End-to-End Implementation:** Design AI systems from prototype to production, including LLM integration, agentic architectures, and RAG implementations.
* **Infrastructure:** Build robust data pipelines and infrastructure supporting AI/ML workloads at scale.
* **Optimization:** Oversee model deployment, fine-tuning, and optimization for production environments.
* **Cloud Architecture:** Architect scalable microservices and cloud-native solutions for real-time AI applications.
* **Governance:** Ensure responsible AI practices, including guardrails, performance monitoring, and ethical considerations.

## REQUIREMENTS
* **Education:** Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
* **Experience:** * 10+ years in software development.
    * 1+ years building **production GenAI solutions** in B2B SaaS environments.
* **Engineering Proficiency:** * **Backend:** Expert-level Python, Java, Go, or Node.js; microservices and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
    * **Frontend:** Strong understanding of React/Next.js and TypeScript; experience with WebSockets and real-time data streaming.
* **AI/ML Expertise:**
    * **Frameworks:** LangChain, LlamaIndex, and APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-source).
    * **Architectures:** Agentic AI (tool calling, memory systems, multi-step reasoning) and RAG systems.
    * **Data:** Vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, ChromaDB) and semantic search.
* **Operational Excellence:**
    * **MLOps:** Model versioning, A/B testing, and lifecycle management in production.
    * **Data Engineering:** Designing ETL processes and pipelines for AI workloads.
* **Collaboration:** Skilled at translating business requirements into technical solutions for cross-functional stakeholders.
